# 📊 Swiggy Data Analysis - Insights

## 🔍 Objective
Analyze restaurant data to understand pricing, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Pricing vs Ratings
Premium-priced restaurants demonstrate higher average ratings, indicating a positive correlation between pricing and perceived quality.

### 2. Delivery Speed Impact
Restaurants with faster delivery times tend to receive higher ratings, suggesting delivery speed is a key driver of customer satisfaction.

---

## 📊 Additional Observations
- Mid-range restaurants dominate the dataset in terms of count
- Delivery efficiency directly impacts customer ratings
- Budget restaurants show more variability in ratings

---

## 🧠 Conclusion
Both pricing strategy and delivery efficiency play a significant role in customer satisfaction and restaurant performance.
